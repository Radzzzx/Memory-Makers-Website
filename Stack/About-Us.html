About Us

Memory makers is a start-up photography studio dedicated to capturing life’s most beautiful moments. Memory Makers is formed by six final year undergraduates aspiring to break through to the Camera and Photography Industry. Based in Birmingham, we aim to specialize in a variety of photography services, including portraits, events and commercial shoots.

At Memory Makers, we believe that every picture should reflect the personality and emotions of our clients and most importantly, CREATE NOSTALGIC MEMORIES that would last a lifetime and more for our clients. As we are only starting-up our company we currently partner with an external photography studio “K.C” located in Birmingham city centre. As we keep expanding, we aim to own our inventory and to establish locations in multiple cities across the U.K. At Memory Makers, we are currently focused on Graduation photoshoots for University Graduates within the West Midlands and as Aston undergraduates we are keen on starting our photoshoots exclusively for Aston graduates and slowly expand to other Universities as we grow as a company.

We endeavour to work closely with graduates to understand their vision, ensuring a personalized experience from start to finish and the creation of our website aims to address this. Our state-of-the-art equipment and innovative techniques from K.C allow us to deliver high-quality images that graduates will cherish for a lifetime. Our talented team of photographers from KC combines artistic vision with technical expertise to create stunning images that tell your unique story. With a passion for storytelling and a commitment to excellence, Memory Makers aims to create moments through pixels that our great graduates would forever cherish. Mission Statement: At Memory Makers, we are dedicated to Creating Nostalgic Memories through stunning visuals. Our mission is to provide a self-personalized and artistic photography service that transforms your unique stories into beautiful, lasting memories. We strive to inspire and connect with our clients, ensuring that each photoshoot reflects their individuality and vision and provide excellence “pixel by pixel”
